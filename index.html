About SkillBase

SkillBase is your trusted platform to find skilled professionals across all trades — electricians, plumbers, painters, cleaners, and more. Unlike conventional services managed by companies, SkillBase empowers independent skilled individuals to connect directly with customers.

Our mission is to create opportunities for every skilled person to showcase their talent, build their reputation, and get hired without middlemen taking the bigger cut.

How It Works

1. Post a Service

Customers post what they need — from home repairs to custom projects.

2. Get Matched

Skilled professionals respond with offers based on your request.

3. Hire & Pay

Choose the best worker, make secure payments, and get the work done.

4. Review & Rate

Share feedback to help grow the worker’s reputation.
